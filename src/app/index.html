What is DigiByte?

DigiByte is a decentralized blockchain platform designed to provide a secure, fast, and scalable environment for various use cases. Launched on January 10, 2014, DigiByte has gained recognition for its commitment to decentralization and security.

What are DigiAssets?

DigiAssets is a secure, scalable layer on top of the DigiByte blockchain that allows for the decentralized issuance of digital assets, tokens, smart contracts, digital identity and more.

What is Digi-ID?

Digi-ID is a security protocol built on DigiByte blockchain technology that empowers users to effortlessly sign-in to websites, applications, and even into the internet of things.

What is DigiByte Desktop?

DigiByte Desktop is a light wallet for desktop operating systems. A multi purpose wallet capable of managing your DigiByte accounts.
